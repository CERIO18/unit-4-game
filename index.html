Crystal Collector Game

Rules of the Game

You will be given a random number (referred to as "Magic Number.") There are four assorted color crystals below. When you select one of the crystals, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the magic number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the magic number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. At the beginning of each game, the game will change the values of each crystal.
